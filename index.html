8 Eligible Zakat Recipients (Asnaf)

1. Al-Fuqarā (Poor)
No income or property. Cannot meet basic needs.
2. Al-Masākīn (Needy)
Have some income but insufficient for needs.
3. Al-Āmilīn (Administrators)
Those collecting/distributing zakat.
4. Al-Mu'allafatu Qulūbuhum
New converts or those whose hearts need reconciling.
5. Ar-Riqāb (Captives)
Freeing slaves/captives (modern interpretations vary).
6. Al-Ghārimīn (Debtors)
Those in debt from halal needs.
7. Fī Sabīlillāh (Allah's Cause)
Jihad, Islamic education, da'wah.
8. Ibn as-Sabīl (Travellers)
Stranded travellers in need.
⚠️ Important: Zakat can ONLY go to these 8 categories. Sadaqah has broader flexibility.
